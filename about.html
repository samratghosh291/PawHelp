About

This website helps you to adopt,list your dog for adoption, find best pet medical services and veterinarian and help to find best petcare for 24x7.

Because we care for your Pet.

Since January 2008, Paw Help's mission has been to improve the health of homeless animals and increase their chances for survival and adoption. We aim to eliminate animal homelessness and euthanasia. We accomplish this by: Donating life-saving pet food, treats, blankets, towels, beds, leashes, collars, harnesses, toys, crates, medical and grooming supplies, and more to shelters, rescue groups, pet pantries, animal transporters, foster caretakers, and wildlife sanctuaries Donating essential pet food and supplies to pets of the homeless and struggling pet owners including the disabled, families, and seniors Donating essential pet food and supplies to veterans working directly with the Veterans Administration Teaching our Humane Education Learning Program (HELP) to children from preschool to high school Working on-call to distribute critical pet food and supplies during natural disasters like floods, earthquakes, and the COVID-19 pandemic Promoting "Adoption is the Best Option" through our Website.
